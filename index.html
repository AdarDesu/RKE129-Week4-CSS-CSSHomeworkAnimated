Making rice

Ingredient Amount
Rice 1 kg
Water 1 l
Salt To taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice:

    Rinse, swirl, then drain the water from the rice a few times with cold water to get rid of extra starch build up from broken grains or other debris. You can wash the rice in the rice cooker pot directly and use your hand to move the rice grains around. Pour out the cloudy water from the pot and repeat if necessary.

  2. Water to rice ratio:

    For white rice it's reccommended to use 1:1 water to rice ratio if you cook in a cooker. If you simply want to cook it in a pot, you can use a bit more water.

  3. Cooking:

    Put the rice in a rice cooker, add water and some salt. Close the cooker lid and start the cooking program. If your cooker is smart enough, it waits automattically after cooking, to let the rice rest. If your cooker doesn't have it in the program, you can let the rice rest for a few minutes after cooking. After that it's ready.
